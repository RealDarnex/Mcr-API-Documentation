Why This Error Occurs

The error 181 is triggered when an attempt is made to kill an entity that is not a LivingEntity. In Minecraft, not all entities represent living creatures. Examples of non-living entities include dropped items, experience orbs, projectiles like arrows, and decorative objects such as armor stands or paintings.

Attempting to perform actions like setting health or invoking combat mechanics on these non-living entities is invalid and therefore results in this error. Always ensure that the entity you are targeting is indeed a LivingEntity before performing any operations that affect health or other life-related attributes.

This error helps prevent unexpected behavior or crashes in your plugin by ensuring that only appropriate entity types are manipulated.
